Let us recommend you some of our best tips if you are planing a trip

1. CHOOSE YOUR PERFECT DESTINATIONS

Australia is bursting with incredible destinations the only question is, which ones to pick? Each Aussie spot offers a unique holiday, so whether you’re a foodie, beach lover or adventurer, there’s a place perfectly suited to you. It's also worth considering the time of year you're planning to visit think school holidays and the weather and how long you've got to explore.

2. PICK A TIME OF YEAR

The next key piece of the planning puzzle is to figure out the best time of year to take your trip. You'll need to take seasons, school holidays and events into consideration, all of which can impact the cost, availability and types of experiences on offer. Discover the best time of year to take your trip.

3. DISCOVER ITINERARIES, BIG AND SMALL

Australia might be big, but there are plenty of itineraries to choose from, no matter how long you've got to explore. You'd be surprised at how much you can see in as little as three to six days, but if you've got more time on your hands, check the different itineraries that will give you a flavour of multiple Australian states and cultures.

4. FIND YOUR PERFECT ACCOMMODATION

Finding the perfect accommodation for your holiday can make all the difference to your trip. Australia has a plethora of accommodation options, ranging from boutique hotels to charming glamping sites and family-friendly resorts.

5. START BUDGETING

Budgeting ahead of time is a sure way to guarantee a low-stress, relaxing holiday, which leads to the ultimate question: how much do I need to budget for my trip? Australian experiences range from free walking tours to lavish experiences. The amount you’ll need to budget really depends on what your dream itinerary looks like, but don’t worry, there are plenty of ways to save money too.

6. USE A PLANNING APP

If you're tired of carrying around a wallet full of travel documents, why not help yourself and the planet at the same time? Go digital by using online apps such as Trello, Tripcase or Tripit where you can store all of your travel information, including itineraries, flight details, car rentals and even your packing lists, all in one place.
