Where can I stay if i go to Australia?

Sidney

  1. Luxury Hotel: InterContinental Sydney, Park Hyatt Sydney.
  2. Boutique Accommodation: Ovolo 1888 Darling Harbour, The Old Clare Hotel.
  3. Hostel: Sydney Harbour YHA, Wake Up! Sydney.

Melbourne

  1. Luxury Hotel: The Langham, Melbourne, Crown Towers.
  2. Boutique Accommodation: The Cullen, The Lyall Hotel and Spa.
  3. Hostel: Melbourne Central YHA, Space Hotel.

Brisbane

  1. Luxury Hotel: Stamford Plaza Brisbane, W Brisbane.
  2. Boutique Accommodation: Spicers Balfour Hotel, The New Inchcolm Hotel & Suites.
  3. Hostel: Brisbane City YHA, BUNK Brisbane.

Cairns (Gateway to the Great Barrier Reef)

  1. Luxury Hotel: Shangri-La Hotel, The Reef House.
  2. Boutique Accommodation: Daintree Eco Lodge, Thala Beach Nature Reserve.
  3. Hostel: Cairns Central YHA, Gilligan's Backpacker Hotel & Resort.

Perth

  1. Luxury Hotel: COMO The Treasury, Crown Metropol Perth.
  2. Boutique Accommodation: Alex Hotel, Tribe Perth.
  3. Hostel: Perth City YHA, Hay Street Traveller's Inn.

Hobart (Tasmania)

  1. Luxury Hotel: MACq 01 Hotel, Henry Jones Art Hotel.
  2. Boutique Accommodation: Islington Hotel, The Corinda Collection.
  3. Hostel: Montacute Boutique Bunkhouse, The Pickled Frog.
